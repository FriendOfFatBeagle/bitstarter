In 2012, there were 2.65 billion customer loyalty memberships. An average of 21.9 memberships per household.

But consumers aren't very loyal. Memberships were active in ONLY 44 percent of those programs. The other inactive 54 percent is a HUGE lost opportunity! (OUCH!)**100

Why?: Restrictions equal mega pain. When loyalty program equals painful relationship, expect divorce!

Time restriction- Points earned may expire monthly, etc

Geographic restriction -Redeemable only at particular store

Purchase restriction- Some items may not apply, ….etc...

Restriction implications: Shoppers throw their loyalty behind a credit card rather than store loyalty.

Why is credit card rewards preferred? Simplicity.

Our solution:

Rise of mobile means consumer behavior has changed.

People are relying on instant communications, more on referrals, recommendations, and online opinions.

Loyalty program must benefit ALL customers also, not just company.

Avoid “groupon-ing” your business. Manage customer reward expectations.

Discard many (stupid) restrictions, and learn to KISS (Keep It Simple Stupid).

Make loyalty program like CASH rewards.

Allow for instant and future gratification reward options. Reward referrals also.

This way, KISS for everybody means happiness to all!

Yes, spread your LOVE (for customer appreciations) wide open.

Results:

Make a little less per customer but sell much much more, and increase customers.

Which means more money in your bank, and a more stable business.
